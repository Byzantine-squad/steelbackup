Stainless Steel based metal backup for your cryptocurrency wallet. Laser marked 12 gauge steel to precisely record up to 33 words (7 characters for each word) from the PGP or BIP39 word lists.

This product is laser marked with a Molybdenum Disulfide based solution. The result is marking that is scratch, acids, and high temperature (up to 650 degrees F) resistant.

- Two Tamper evident bags
